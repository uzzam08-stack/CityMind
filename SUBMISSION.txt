CityMind is a hyper-local, AI-powered Decision Intelligence Platform built for the Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC). Addressing Problem Statement 2 through the enterprise architecture of Problem Statement 1, we tackled the specific bottleneck of reactive Construction & Demolition (C&D) waste management. 

Currently, city managers rely on passive data. CityMind bridges the gap between raw spatial telemetry and physical action. We engineered a closed-loop system that ingests simulated C&D waste data (grounded in actual PCMC factsheets and transfer station coordinates). When an anomaly like illegal dumping is flagged, the platform actively responds. 

To handle the massive spatial calculations required for PCMC's 9,700+ registered construction zones, we integrated NVIDIA RAPIDS cuDF. This zero-code-change GPU acceleration dropped our Haversine routing matrix latency from 8.4 seconds to 0.6 seconds—a 14x speedup that allows for real-time automated truck dispatch. 

Additionally, we implemented a Gemini 1.5 Flash multimodal pipeline. Ward commissioners can upload images of illegal dumping for automated volume estimation and statutory penalty calculation, or use our RAG-powered chat interface to query BigQuery databases using natural language. CityMind doesn't just display data; it uses Google Cloud and NVIDIA hardware to make intelligent municipal decisions in real-time.
